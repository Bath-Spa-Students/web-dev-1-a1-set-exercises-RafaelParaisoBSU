Plot

Dragon Ball Super: Broly" follows Goku and Vegeta encountering Broly, a Saiyan survivor with immense power. Unleashing his fury, Broly engages in a titanic battle with the Saiyan warriors, revealing his tragic past intertwined with the destruction of Planet Vegeta. As the conflict escalates, old wounds resurface, and secrets are unveiled. The film explores themes of power, family, and redemption amidst breathtaking action sequences. Through intense combat and emotional depth, "Dragon Ball Super: Broly" weaves a gripping narrative, ultimately reshaping the Dragon Ball universe with the introduction of a formidable new foe and the potential for unexpected alliances.
